"Captain <>Corelli</>’s Mandolin is set in the early days of the second world war, before <>Benito Mussolini</> invaded Greece. Dr <>Iannis</> practices medicine on the island of Cephalonia, accompanied by his daughter, <>Pelagia</>, to whom he imparts much of his healing art. Even when the Italians do invade, life isn’t so bad—at first anyway."
