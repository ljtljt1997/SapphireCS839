"<>Babbitt</> is set in the modern Midwestern city of Zenith. <>George F. Babbitt</>, a 46- year-old real estate broker, enjoys all the modern conveniences available to a prosperous middle-class businessman, yet he is dissatisfied with his life. When the novel opens, <>Babbitt</> has begun to regularly indulge in fantasies about a fairy girl who makes him feel like a gallant youth."
